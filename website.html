About Me

Hi! My name is Kartiki Sharma and I am passionate about Design and Technology. For this reason, I am continually seeking for more knowledge and developing my skills in Web & App Development. I also like working with children and taking on volunteer tasks which educate and encourage them to pursue their interests.
